FAQ

©

[01]
What services does Convix offer?

We provide end-to-end creative and digital solutions tailored to your needs. Our core services include Web Design & Development, Brand Identity, UI/UX Design, Motion & Creative Direction

[02]
How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines depend on the scope and complexity of your needs. On average: Web Design & Development: 3–6 weeks Brand Identity: 2–4 weeks UI/UX Design: 2–5 weeks Custom Projects: Timelines vary based on requirements

[03]
Do you provide custom design or use templates?

We focus on creating custom designs tailored to your brand, goals, and audience. While templates can speed up certain projects, our priority is delivering unique solutions that stand out and align perfectly with your vision.

[04]
What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is flexible and depends on the scope, complexity, and timeline of your project. We typically work on: Fixed-price packages for defined projects (e.g., websites, branding). Custom quotes tailored to your unique requirements.

[05]
How do revisions and feedback work during a project?

We follow a collaborative process where your feedback is encouraged at every stage. Typically, we include 2–3 revision rounds within each project phase to refine the design until it meets your vision. Feedback is shared through clear review links, and we make adjustments promptly to keep the project moving smoothly.

[06]
Can you help with branding and strategy, not just design?

Yes! Along with design, we also help shape your brand identity and strategy. This includes positioning, visual direction, messaging, and creating a cohesive identity that connects with your audience. Our goal is not just to make your brand look good—but to make it work effectively for your business.

[07]
How do I get started with your team?

Getting started is simple! Just reach out through our contact form or email with your project details. We’ll schedule a quick consultation to understand your goals, suggest the best approach, and provide a custom proposal. Once you’re ready, we’ll guide you step by step from onboarding to project launch.
